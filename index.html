Why our output doesn't look like AI slop

An anti-slop standard, enforced by a critic who can say no

🎯

Taste, on rails

Every frame, cut, and sound cue is a deliberate choice. We actively reject the AI tells — default voices, stock slideshows, dead-air endings, karaoke captions — that make most AI video forgettable.

⛔

A gate that bends for no one

A dedicated critic scores the work against a locked rubric. An 8/10 is non-negotiable, and it happens before the expensive step — so we never polish a bad idea.

⏱

Timely or it doesn't ship

For anything trend- or news-driven, we check freshness in your timezone first. A day-late daily recap is dead on arrival — so we ship same-day or reframe it.
